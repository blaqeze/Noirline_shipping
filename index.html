Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our shipping services.

How does the Telegram booking process work?
Our booking process is simple: 1) Select a service on our website, 2) Click "Book Now" to message our agent on Telegram, 3) Provide shipment details to the agent, 4) Receive a personalized quote and shipping label, 5) Track your shipment through our portal.
What information should I provide to the agent?
Please provide: Package dimensions and weight, contents description, origin and destination addresses, desired service level, and any special handling requirements. Our agent will guide you through the process.
How quickly will the agent respond?
Our shipping specialists typically respond within 15 minutes during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM local time, Monday-Friday). For after-hours inquiries, you'll receive a response the next business day.
Can I get a quote before committing?
Absolutely! Our agent will provide a detailed, no-obligation quote based on your shipment details. This quote includes all costs - shipping, insurance, and any applicable customs fees.
How do I pay for shipping services?
After confirming your shipment details, our agent will provide secure payment options including bank transfer, PayPal, or other local payment methods based on your location.
